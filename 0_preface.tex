\noindent
This report was written as part of the course \textit{IT2901 Prosjektarbeid Informatikk II} at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (NTNU).
The task, assigned by GE Healthcare and Capgemini, was to research the possibility of creating a secure mobile application able to upload ultrasound images to any given hospital, regardless of the system in use. Based on the results from the research, an Android application was developed.
